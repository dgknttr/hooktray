# Code of Conduct

HookTray is a community-first project. We want the project to be useful, welcoming, and respectful for people with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the technical problem.
- Help newcomers understand the codebase and contribution process.
- Respect privacy and do not share other people's sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces
- Dismissing security or privacy concerns without review

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently limit participation in project spaces.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainer directly through the contact method listed on the repository profile.
